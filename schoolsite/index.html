The MEHMS 7th Grade Curriculum

Mary Ellen Henderson is a middle school in Falls Church, VA that is currently trying to implement a stem curriculum into their classrooms. This is a site showcasing their 7th grade curriculum

Tweets by @mehms

Science

In science this year we are learning about how cells and animals work. Specifically, recently we have started learning about passive and active transport within cells. Some of the stuff she is teaching us is on a 9th grade level!

Math

This year in math we are learning Algebra. This class is two years advanced for my grade! Currently we are learning how to do inequalities. After that we will learn substitution, an important component of algebra.

Civics

This year in civics we are learning about GOV. and how to be a responsible global citizen. Right now we are learning about how courts and trials work. We get to roleplay as attorneys and witnesses and write opening and closing statements.

English

This year we are learning a lot of things relating to grammar and writing. Right now we are learning about utopias and dystopia. We got to write speeches and stories about a world we create! We also learned the basics of the dystopia genre.
